Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

Murder Board ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile application developed by Terror LLC that provides AI-powered murder mystery detective game experiences. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Murder Board is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Game Progress: Your case history, investigation notes, interrogation transcripts, achievements, and game statistics are stored locally on your device and synced via iCloud. This data is never transmitted to our servers except for AI-powered interrogation features (see below).
  • AI Interrogation Data: When you interrogate suspects, your questions and the AI-generated responses are processed through our secure Supabase backend using Anthropic's Claude API. These conversations are temporarily processed to generate contextual suspect responses and are not stored long-term or used for AI training.
  • Daily Mystery Cases: Daily mystery cases are generated server-side using AI and delivered to your device. No personal information is required to receive these cases.
  • iCloud Sync: If you enable iCloud on your device, your game progress (cases, notes, stats) is automatically synced across your Apple devices using Apple's iCloud service. We do not have access to this data.
  • Anonymous Analytics: We collect anonymous usage data to improve app stability and performance. This includes a randomly generated device identifier, device model, operating system version, app session duration, screen views, and crash/error reports. This data is not linked to your identity and cannot be used to identify you personally.

How We Use Your Information

  • To generate contextual AI-powered suspect responses during interrogations
  • To deliver daily mystery cases to your device
  • To sync your game progress across your Apple devices via iCloud
  • To improve app stability and performance through anonymous analytics

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Backend infrastructure for AI interrogation features and daily case delivery. Interrogation data is processed in real-time and not stored long-term. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic Claude: AI language model used to generate suspect interrogation responses. Conversations are processed ephemerally and not used for model training. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple iCloud: Game progress sync across your devices (optional). See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

Game progress data (cases, notes, stats) is stored locally on your device and optionally synced via iCloud. Interrogation questions and responses are processed in real-time through our secure backend and are not stored long-term. Anonymous analytics data is stored securely on our infrastructure. If you delete the app, all locally stored data is removed.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Data is only shared with the third-party services listed above as necessary to provide app functionality (AI interrogations and daily case delivery).

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all network communication with our backend and third-party services. Interrogation data is processed over secure connections and not retained long-term.

Children's Privacy

Murder Board is rated 12+ for mild violence and mature themes. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
