Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

Punchcard ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile application developed by Terror LLC that helps hourly workers track shifts, hours, and earnings. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Punchcard is designed with privacy as a core principle. All your data stays on your device:

  • Subscription Information: If you subscribe to Punchcard Pro, purchase and subscription status is managed through RevenueCat and Apple's in-app purchase system. We do not have access to your payment details.
  • App Data: Your jobs, shifts, hours, earnings calculations, and app preferences are stored locally on your device using on-device storage. This data is never transmitted to our servers.

  • We do not transmit your shift or earnings data to any server

How We Use Your Information

  • To manage your subscription status and provide premium features
  • To store your jobs, shifts, and preferences locally on your device

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: Subscription and in-app purchase management. RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase data. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple In-App Purchases: Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

All app data (jobs, shifts, hours, earnings, preferences) is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal data. If you delete the app, all locally stored data is removed.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Data is only shared with the third-party services listed above as necessary to provide app functionality.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for any network communication with third-party services.

Children's Privacy

Punchcard is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
