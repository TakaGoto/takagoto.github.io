Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Pulse ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile application developed by Terror LLC that provides habit tracking and streak monitoring. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Pulse is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Subscription Information: If you subscribe to Pulse Premium, purchase and subscription status is managed through RevenueCat and Apple's in-app purchase system. We do not have access to your payment details.
  • App Preferences: Your settings, habits, completion history, and streak data are stored locally on your device using on-device storage. This data is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Notification Permission: Pulse may request permission to send local notifications for daily habit reminders. Notification tokens are managed locally on your device and are not sent to any server.
  • Anonymous Analytics: We collect anonymous usage data to improve app stability and performance. This includes a randomly generated device identifier, device model, operating system version, app session duration, screen views, and crash/error reports. This data is not linked to your identity and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: Subscription and in-app purchase management. RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase data. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple In-App Purchases: Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. See Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase: Anonymous analytics and crash reporting infrastructure. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

All app data (settings, habits, completion history, streak data, preferences) is stored locally on your device. We do not store your personal app data on our servers. Anonymous analytics data is stored securely on our infrastructure. If you delete the app, all locally stored data is removed.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Data is only shared with the third-party services listed above as necessary to provide app functionality.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for any network communication with third-party services.

Children's Privacy

Pulse is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
